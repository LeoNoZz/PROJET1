The history of the 24h of Le Mans Moto

Discover the evolution of the race since its beginnings.

The 24 Heures Motos has a long and rich history, dating back to 1912 with the first motorcycle race organized at Le Mans. The event was organized by the Automobile Club de l'Ouest (ACO), which also organizes the famous 24 Hours of Le Mans for cars. The 24 Heures Motos was first raced in 1978, and has become a very popular motorcycle endurance race. The race takes place on the Bugatti circuit in Le Mans, and is an event of the FIM Endurance World Championship (EWC). The 24 Heures Motos is a difficult and demanding race, which tests the riders, the bikes and the teams. It is a major event for the motorcycle world, and attracts fans from all over the world.
